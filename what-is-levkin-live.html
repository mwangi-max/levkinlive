What is Levkin Live?

Levkin Live is an entity that deals with developing, designing, and maintaining online content, be it websites, online marketing activities e.g., Facebook Marketing amongst others.
We tap on the immense opportunity being provided by the internet to reach your clients and/or customers wherever they may be. That is the goal of levkin live - connecting you with the world.
With the accelerated pace of technological advancements, and being exacerbated by the current pandemic that is ravaging the globe, Covid19, whereby movement is highly restricted in some places due to the stay at home orders, it is only prudent that businesses adopt and establish themselves on this advancement so as to leverage on it. And Levkin Live is here to facilitate your business take advantage of this because as statistics clearly shows the market place is shifting rapidly to the online spectrum. As of January 2021, there were 4.66 billion active internet users worldwide - 59.5 percent of the global population. Of this total, 92.6 percent (4.32 billion) accessed the internet via mobile devices, as reported by Statisa. And going by these statistics, we can say that the world has become a global village, and as the adage goes, 'statistics do not lie'. Marketers, producers, and businesses amongst other now has a wider market as compared to before the online advancements. Today you cannot rely only on the traditional media e.g., radio, print, and TV to pass information to your consumers. Most, if not all of your clients/customers are on the internet and not forgetting the many potential ones that you can 'fish' there. To do this you need to have a stable online presence and that's why Levkin Live is here to facilitate you on this.
We do 'visualize' you on the net and make the globe your stage. And while at it, we do this affordably so that we can give you a peace of mind to concentrate on the other important things. Our content will engage with your clients in regards to your objectives. No other place to get this kind of services apart from Levkin Live. We have various packages in our service catalogue and it will be good if you can sample them to find your specific item, they are classified into two broad categories, SHORTS which are basically short projects to be undertaken in the course of one week, and the LONGS, which are long-term projects which takes more than one week to accomplish the objectives.
