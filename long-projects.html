Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Search Engine Optimization, popularly known as SEO, is a technique whereby an existing or upcoming website is designed or to put it simply, organized in such a way that people can be able to see it online when they are doing their searching for information in search engines like Google.

Keywords or the main points are put in a prime position so as to be indexed or seen by the search engines, upping the chances of your content being seen online.
